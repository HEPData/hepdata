Advanced Search Tips

When you search on a word, e.g. 'collisions', we will automatically search across everything we store about a record. But, sometimes you may wish to be more specific. Here we show you how.

Guidance on the query string syntax can be found in the Elasticsearch documentation.

Search on title or abstract

  • Find all data with collisions in the title
    • title:collisions

  • Find all data with "lepton-jet resonances" in the abstract
    • abstract:"lepton-jet resonances"
      Unless the string is in quotes, we will search for all records with lepton-jet OR resonances in the abstract. Quotes force a full match.

Search by keywords

  • Find all data with the phrase injection or "elastic scattering"
    • phrases:injection OR phrases:"elastic scattering"

  • Find all data with the P P --> LQ LQ X reaction
    • reactions:"P P --> LQ LQ X"

  • Find data by CM Energy (in GeV):
    • cmenergies:1.34
    • cmenergies:[1.3 TO 1.4] (inclusive range, i.e. 1.3 ≤ √ s ≤ 1.4)
    • cmenergies:{1.3 TO 1.4} (exclusive range, i.e. 1.3 < √ s < 1.4)
    • cmenergies:[1.3 TO 1.4} (half-open range, i.e. 1.3 ≤ √ s < 1.4)

  • Find all data with observable ASYM
    • observables:ASYM

Other useful searches

  • Find all data with neutron energy in the title AND the CM energy of 7000 GeV
    • title:"neutron energy" AND cmenergies:7000.0

  • Find all data from the collaborations D0 OR ATLAS
    • collaborations:"D0" OR collaborations:"ATLAS"

  • Find all data where there is an author from Fermilab.
    • authors.affiliation:"Fermilab"

  • All papers from year 2015 from the collaboration LHCf with the reaction P P --> N X.
    • year:2015 AND collaborations:LHCf AND reactions:"P P --> N X"

  • Find a record by the Inspire record number, journal DOI, or HEPData DOI.
    • inspire_id:513337
    • doi:"10.1007/s100520000432"
    • hepdata_doi:"10.17182/hepdata.12882"
    Note that the DOI strings need to be enclosed in quotation marks.
    The field names can be omitted as they will be queried in a full-text search.
    • 513337
    • "10.1007/s100520000432"
    • "10.17182/hepdata.12882"

Searching via Inspire

  • Search Inspire for records that have a corresponding HEPData entry.
    • external_system_identifiers.schema:HEPData
